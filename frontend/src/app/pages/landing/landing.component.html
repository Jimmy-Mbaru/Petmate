FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the PetMate adoption process.

How does the PetMate matching process work?

Our matching process starts with a detailed questionnaire about your lifestyle, home environment, and preferences. Our AI-powered algorithm then analyzes 50+ factors to find pets that match your unique criteria. Once we identify potential matches, we facilitate introductions and support you through the entire adoption journey.

What is the adoption timeline?

The timeline varies depending on your preferences and availability. On average, most families find their perfect match within 2-4 weeks. The process includes completing an application, meeting potential pets, and a home visit. We prioritize quality matches over speed to ensure lasting bonds.

Are all pets vaccinated and health-checked?

Yes, absolutely. All pets in our network are fully vaccinated, spayed/neutered, and receive comprehensive health examinations before being listed. We provide complete medical records and vaccination history for every pet, ensuring transparency and peace of mind.

What support do you provide after adoption?

We're with you every step of the way! Post-adoption support includes regular check-ins, access to our 24/7 expert support team, training resources, and a community of fellow pet parents. We also offer guidance on nutrition, behavior, and veterinary care to ensure a smooth transition.

What are the adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary by pet and typically range from $150 to $500. These fees help cover veterinary care, vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and microchipping. Our PetMate service fee is included in this cost, covering our comprehensive matching and support services.
